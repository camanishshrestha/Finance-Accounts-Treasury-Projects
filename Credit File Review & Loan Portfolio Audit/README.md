## 🎯 Project Objective

Credit is one of the **highest-risk areas in banking**, requiring strict governance and documentation discipline.

This project focused on reviewing loan files and credit portfolios to evaluate whether lending decisions were:
- properly appraised  
- correctly approved  
- fully documented  
- adequately secured  
- correctly classified and provisioned  

> ⚠️ Confidentiality Note: Borrower-specific data and sensitive credit details are excluded.

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Work Performed

---

### ✅ Step 1: Loan File Selection

- Obtained full loan portfolio listing
- Selected samples based on risk factors:
  - large exposures  
  - overdue accounts  
  - restructured loans  
  - renewed facilities  
  - sector concentration  

- Ensured coverage across multiple products and branches

---

### ✅ Step 2: Credit Appraisal Review

Evaluated whether lending decisions were properly supported by analysis:

- borrower background assessment  
- income/business evaluation  
- financial statement review  
- repayment capacity analysis  
- cash flow assessment  
- loan purpose validation  
- credit bureau / CIB checks  

Checked whether approvals were **data-driven and justified**

---

### ✅ Step 3: Approval Hierarchy Verification

- Verified sanction authority compliance
- Reviewed:
  - approval notes  
  - committee decisions  
  - delegated authority limits  

- Identified any deviations from approval hierarchy

---

### ✅ Step 4: Documentation Completeness Check

Checked loan files for:

- application forms  
- KYC documents  
- loan agreements  
- collateral documents  
- guarantor information  
- insurance records  
- valuation reports  
- renewal documents  

Ensured files were complete, valid, and properly maintained

---

### ✅ Step 5: Collateral Verification

Assessed adequacy and legality of security:

- collateral valuation accuracy  
- legal documentation completeness  
- charge registration status  
- insurance validity  
- collateral coverage vs exposure  

Identified issues like:
- outdated valuation  
- missing insurance  
- incomplete charge creation  

---

### ✅ Step 6: Disbursement Control Testing

- Verified compliance before loan disbursement
- Checked:
  - condition precedent fulfillment  
  - approval before release  
  - proper disbursement channel  
  - end-use controls (where applicable)  

Highlighted weak pre-disbursement control cases

---

### ✅ Step 7: Loan Classification & Provisioning Review

- Verified loan classification accuracy:
  - pass  
  - watchlist  
  - substandard  
  - doubtful  
  - loss  

- Checked provisioning adequacy
- Compared system classification with actual file condition
- Identified misclassification and delayed downgrades

---

### ✅ Step 8: Renewal & Monitoring Review

- Reviewed renewable credit facilities
- Checked:
  - timely renewal  
  - updated financials  
  - stock statements  
  - business review reports  

- Evaluated post-disbursement monitoring effectiveness

---

### ✅ Step 9: Portfolio Risk Analysis

- Identified portfolio-level patterns:
  - sector concentration risk  
  - recurring documentation issues  
  - collateral gaps  
  - renewal delays  
  - overdue trends  

Determined whether issues were **systemic or isolated**

---

### ✅ Step 10: Reporting & Recommendations

Prepared structured audit findings:

Key issues identified:
- weak credit appraisal support  
- incomplete documentation  
- delayed renewals  
- collateral deficiencies  
- classification inconsistencies  

Recommendations focused on:
- stronger appraisal discipline  
- improved credit monitoring  
- tighter documentation control  
- better provisioning accuracy  
- improved portfolio oversight
