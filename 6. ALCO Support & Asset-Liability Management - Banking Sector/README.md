| Field | Details |

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To support the bank’s Asset-Liability Committee (ALCO) in making data-driven decisions for balance sheet optimization, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

Key focus areas:
- Interest rate risk management
- Liquidity risk monitoring
- Cost of funds optimization
- Balance sheet structure analysis
- ALCO decision tracking and implementation

---

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities

### 1. ALCO Information Pack Preparation
- Developed monthly ALCO dashboards from CBS, Treasury, Credit, and Risk data
- Covered balance sheet structure, liquidity, funding, and risk indicators
- Provided decision-ready analytical reports for management

---

### 2. Interest Rate Risk Analysis
- Repricing gap analysis (RSA vs RSL)
- NII sensitivity under interest rate shock scenarios
- EVE (Economic Value of Equity) impact analysis

---

### 3. Liquidity Risk Management
- Structural liquidity gap analysis across maturity buckets
- Monitoring of CRR, SLR, LCR, and NSFR compliance
- Early warning identification for liquidity stress

---

### 4. Cost of Funds & Profitability Analysis
- Weighted Average Cost of Funds (WACF)
- Yield on Assets (WAYA)
- Net Interest Margin (NIM) tracking
- Deposit and funding cost optimization insights

---

### 5. Balance Sheet Optimization
- Analysis of asset-liability mix
- Deposit concentration and funding stability review
- Credit-to-deposit ratio monitoring

---

### 6. ALCO Minutes & Governance Support
- Drafted official ALCO meeting minutes
- Documented decisions, responsibilities, and timelines
- Maintained ALCO action tracker for implementation follow-up

---

### 7. Decision Implementation Tracking
- Coordinated execution of ALCO decisions across departments
- Ensured updates in CBS, Treasury, and rate structures
- Monitored completion of action items

---

### 8. Board & Regulatory Reporting
- Prepared quarterly ALCO summary reports for Board of Directors
- Supported NRB regulatory compliance and inspections
- Ensured alignment between internal and regulatory reporting

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes

- Established structured ALCO reporting framework
- Improved visibility of liquidity and interest rate risks
- Strengthened balance sheet decision-making process
- Ensured timely execution of ALCO decisions
- Enhanced regulatory compliance and governance reporting
