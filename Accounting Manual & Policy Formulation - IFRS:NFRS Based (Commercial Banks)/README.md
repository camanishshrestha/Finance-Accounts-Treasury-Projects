## 🎯 Project Objective

When Nepal transitioned to IFRS/NFRS, banks needed a **standardized accounting manual** to ensure consistent financial reporting across all branches and teams.

My role involved contributing to the development of **IFRS/NFRS-based accounting manuals and policies** for commercial banks to ensure:
- Standardized accounting treatment
- IFRS compliance
- NRB regulatory alignment
- Consistent journal entry application

⚠️ Note: All institution-specific data remains confidential. Only methodology is shared.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Work

---

### ✅ Step 1: Gap Analysis (Local GAAP vs IFRS)

- Reviewed existing accounting policies
- Mapped gaps against IFRS requirements:
  - IFRS 9 replacing IAS 39
  - IFRS 16 Lease accounting
  - IFRS 15 Revenue recognition
- Categorized:
  - Full rewrite required
  - Partial modification
  - New policy requirement

---

### ✅ Step 2: Chart of Accounts (CoA) Design

Designed IFRS-aligned CoA structure:

| Code | Category |
|---|---|
| 1XXXX | Assets |
| 2XXXX | Liabilities |
| 3XXXX | Equity |
| 4XXXX | Income |
| 5XXXX | Expenses |
| 6XXXX | OCI |
| 7XXXX | Off-Balance Sheet |

Key features:
- IFRS + NRB dual mapping
- Hierarchical structure
- Financial statement alignment
- Expandable design

---

### ✅ Step 3: IFRS 9 Financial Instruments Policy

Covered full IFRS 9 lifecycle:

- Classification (AC / FVOCI / FVTPL)
- Business model test
- SPPI test
- ECL (Expected Credit Loss)
- Stage 1–3 impairment model
- Fair value measurement
- Derecognition rules

Included:
- Policy statements
- Journal entries
- Worked examples

---

### ✅ Step 4: IAS 19 Employee Benefits

Covered:
- Short-term benefits
- Defined contribution plans
- Defined benefit obligations (gratuity)
- Actuarial gains/losses (OCI treatment)

Included:
- Actuarial assumptions
- Journal templates
- Disclosure requirements

---

### ✅ Step 5: IAS 12 Income Tax

Covered:
- Current tax accounting
- Deferred tax (asset & liability)
- Temporary differences:

  - Depreciation differences
  - Staff loans
  - Fair value adjustments
