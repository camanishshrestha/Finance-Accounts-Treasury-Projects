## 🎯 Project Objective

Reinsurance and reserves are the **core financial safeguards** of an insurance company:

- **Reinsurance** → protects against large losses  
- **Reserves** → ensure future claims can be paid  

Weakness here = **direct insolvency risk**.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: What I Did

---

### ✅ Step 1: Reinsurance Understanding

- Key concepts reviewed:
  - Retention (what company keeps)
  - Cession (what is transferred)
  - Recovery (what is claimed back)

- Treaty types:
  - Quota Share → fixed %
  - Surplus → excess over retention
  - Excess of Loss → large loss protection

---

### ✅ Step 2: Audit Planning

**Main risks:**

| Area | Risk |
|---|---|
| Cession | Missing / wrong cession |
| Premium | Wrong calculation |
| Recovery | Under-recovery |
| Reserves | Understatement |

---

### ✅ Step 3: Cession Accuracy

- Verified:
  - Retention limits
  - Sum at risk calculation
  - Correct cession amount
  - Proper treaty usage

- Identified:
  - ❌ Policies not ceded (major risk)
  - ❌ Over-cession (extra cost)

---

### ✅ Step 4: Reinsurance Premium

- Checked:
  - Premium = Ceded risk × rate
  - Timely payment to reinsurer
  - FX handling (USD cases)

- Reconciled payable balances

---

### ✅ Step 5: Reinsurance Recovery

- Verified:
  - Claim was ceded
  - Recovery amount correct
  - Recovery received on time

- Flagged:
  - Delayed recoveries
  - Outstanding receivables

---

### ✅ Step 6: Accounting Check

- Verified entries:

| Transaction | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Premium | Expense |
| Recovery | Reduce claim cost |
| Commission | Income |

- Ensured:
  - No netting (gross reporting)
  - Proper disclosures

---

### ✅ Step 7: Reinsurer Reconciliation

- Matched:
  - Company vs reinsurer statements

- Issues found:
  - Timing gaps
  - FX differences
  - Disputed balances

---

### ✅ Step 8: Reserve Adequacy

- Reviewed reserves:

| Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Mathematical | Future liabilities |
| IBNR | Not yet reported claims |
| Outstanding | Reported not paid |

- Checked:
  - Actuarial assumptions (mortality, discount rate)
  - Data accuracy
  - Reserve movement logic
  - Solvency ratio

---

### ✅ Step 9: Premium Audit

- Verified:
  - First premium before issuance
  - Renewal tracking
  - Lapse handling
  - Commission correctness

- Flagged:
  - Churning risk (agent misuse)

---

### ✅ Step 10: Reporting

- Key findings:
  - 🔴 Missing cessions (critical)
  - 🟡 Reconciliation gaps
  - 🟢 Timing delays

- Recommendations:
  - Automate cession triggers
  - Monthly reconciliation
  - Review retention annually
  - Strengthen actuarial validation
