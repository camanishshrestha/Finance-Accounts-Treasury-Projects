## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities

### 1. Business Understanding
- Analyzed revenue streams and cost structures  
- Reviewed regulatory environment (NTA, NERC, PPA frameworks)  

### 2. Audit Planning & Risk Assessment
- Identified key risks:  
  - Telecom: revenue complexity, billing systems, spectrum license  
  - Hydropower: fixed assets, PPA revenue, depreciation  
- Assisted in materiality and audit planning  

### 3. Revenue Audit

#### NCELL (Telecom)
- Verified prepaid and postpaid revenue recognition  
- Checked deferred revenue and billing accuracy  
- Reconciled interconnect revenue  
- Performed ARPU and trend analysis  

#### Himal Power (Hydropower)
- Verified PPA-based revenue (Units × Tariff)  
- Checked NEA confirmations and receivables  
- Reviewed seasonal revenue patterns  

### 4. Fixed Asset Audit

#### Hydropower Plant
- Verified asset register and capitalization  
- Checked depreciation and useful life  
- Reviewed CWIP and impairment indicators  

#### Telecom Infrastructure
- Verified towers, network equipment  
- Checked spectrum license (intangible asset)  
- Reviewed IFRS 16 lease accounting  

### 5. Expense Verification
- Vouched expenses to supporting documents  
- Verified cut-off and accruals  
- Checked royalties and government fees  

### 6. Tax Audit
- Reviewed corporate tax computation  
- Verified deferred tax (IAS 12)  
- Assessed tax disputes and contingencies  
- Checked TDS compliance  

### 7. Other Procedures
- Telecom: subscriber data, inventory, receivables  
- Hydropower: loans, insurance, environmental compliance  

### 8. Audit Completion & Reporting
- Performed subsequent events and going concern review  
- Assisted in audit report and management letter  
- Documented audit working papers
