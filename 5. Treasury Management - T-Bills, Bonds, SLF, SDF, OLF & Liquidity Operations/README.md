# Project 5: Treasury Management — T-Bills, Bonds, SLF, SDF, OLF & Liquidity Operations

## 📌 Overview
This project covers end-to-end treasury operations for two development banks in Nepal (2022–2025), including government securities, money market operations, NRB standing facilities, FX management, and liquidity risk control.

## 🎯 Objective
- Ensure optimal liquidity management and regulatory compliance  
- Maximize returns on surplus funds within risk limits  
- Manage government securities and money market operations  
- Support ALCO decision-making with treasury insights  

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed
- Managed daily liquidity position and CRR/SLR compliance  
- Executed NRB Treasury Bill auction participation and portfolio management  
- Managed Development Bond investments and IFRS 9 accounting  
- Operated NRB standing facilities (SLF, SDF, OLF) for liquidity balancing  
- Handled interbank lending and borrowing operations  
- Managed deposit auctions and short-term liquidity instruments  
- Monitored FX positions and Nostro reconciliations  
- Maintained investment portfolio under IFRS (FVTPL/FVOCI/AC)  
- Prepared treasury risk and ALCO reports (liquidity, interest rate, FX risk)  
- Ensured proper treasury accounting and CBS reconciliation  

## 📊 Key Outcomes
- Zero regulatory breaches in CRR/SLR compliance  
- Efficient liquidity management across daily operations  
- Optimized yield from government securities portfolio  
- Strong ALCO reporting and treasury decision support  
- Improved treasury risk monitoring and controls
