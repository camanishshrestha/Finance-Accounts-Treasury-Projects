## 🎯 Project Objective

As Team Leader, I conducted branch operational audits across **60+ branches nationwide** for a commercial banking engagement.

The objective was to evaluate whether branch operations were being executed with:
- Proper internal controls  
- Regulatory compliance  
- Accurate financial handling  
- Adequate documentation discipline  

and to identify operational gaps and recommend improvements.

> ⚠️ Confidentiality Note: Branch-specific findings and sensitive data are excluded. This focuses on audit methodology and responsibilities only.

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Work Performed

---

### ✅ Step 1: Planned Branch Audit Visits

- Prepared branch-wise audit schedules
- Prioritized branches based on:
  - transaction volume  
  - operational size  
  - risk profile  
  - prior audit issues  
  - geographic factors  

- Reviewed previous audit reports before visits
- Developed branch-specific checklists

---

### ✅ Step 2: Reviewed End-to-End Branch Operations

Assessed key operational areas:
- Account opening process  
- Teller and cash operations  
- Payment and receipt processing  
- Clearing operations  
- Locker management  
- Approval workflows  

Verified whether operations aligned with bank policies and procedures.

---

### ✅ Step 3: Verified Cash Management Controls

- Conducted physical cash counts
- Verified:
  - vault balances  
  - teller cash holdings  
  - joint custody controls  
  - excess cash handling  
  - surprise cash readiness  

- Reconciled physical cash with system records
- Identified discrepancies and control weaknesses

---

### ✅ Step 4: Reviewed KYC / AML Compliance

- Checked customer account files for completeness:
  - identification documents  
  - account opening forms  
  - risk classification  
  - supporting records  

- Evaluated compliance with KYC/AML requirements
- Identified missing documentation and control gaps

---

### ✅ Step 5: Reviewed Reconciliations & Pending Items

- Examined:
  - inter-branch accounts  
  - suspense accounts  
  - sundry balances  
  - outstanding entries  

- Verified:
  - timeliness of reconciliation  
  - supervisory review  
  - resolution of long-pending items  

- Highlighted unresolved balances requiring action

---

### ✅ Step 6: Assessed Documentation Standards

- Reviewed filing systems and record maintenance
- Checked:
  - voucher filing  
  - register maintenance  
  - document traceability  
  - authorization records  

- Identified weak documentation and storage practices

---

### ✅ Step 7: Verified Fixed Assets & Physical Controls

- Physically verified branch assets:
  - IT equipment  
  - furniture  
  - safes  
  - operational devices  

- Checked:
  - asset presence  
  - tagging and tracking  
  - usage condition  

- Observed physical security controls:
  - access restrictions  
  - key management  
  - record security  

---

### ✅ Step 8: Evaluated Operational Control Discipline

- Reviewed:
  - maker-checker compliance  
  - approval hierarchy  
  - exception handling  
  - daily balancing routines  
  - supervisory oversight  

- Identified deviations from standard operating procedures

---

### ✅ Step 9: Documented Findings & Discussed with Management

- Prepared structured audit observations per branch
- Validated findings with branch management
- Categorized issues by risk level
- Ensured factual accuracy before reporting
- Provided actionable recommendations

---

### ✅ Step 10: Consolidated Reporting & Follow-Up

- Compiled branch-level reports into consolidated audit outputs
- Identified recurring themes:
  - KYC gaps  
  - reconciliation delays  
  - cash control weaknesses  
  - documentation issues  

- Supported follow-up on previously raised issues
- Highlighted systemic vs isolated control weaknesses
