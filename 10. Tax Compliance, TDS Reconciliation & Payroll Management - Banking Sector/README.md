## 🎯 Project Objective

Tax compliance in the banking sector involves both **tax deduction on behalf of the government (TDS)** and **corporate tax obligations**. A single error in computation or delay in remittance can lead to penalties and regulatory scrutiny.

My role ensured **end-to-end tax accuracy, payroll compliance, and timely statutory filings** across all tax obligations of the bank.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities

---

### 1. TDS Management (Multi-category)

Handled TDS on:
- Deposit interest
- Employee salaries
- Contractors and vendors
- Rent and professional services
- Commission and fees
- Debenture interest

**Responsibilities:**
- Computed TDS using CBS (Pumori / Finacle)
- Verified PAN-based tax applicability
- Corrected system computation errors
- Prepared monthly TDS returns
- Remitted TDS to IRD via challans

**Outcome:** 100% timely compliance with zero penalties

---

### 2. Payroll Processing & Salary Tax

Managed full payroll lifecycle:
- Salary computation (basic, allowances, bonuses)
- Progressive income tax slab calculation
- Monthly TDS deduction
- Year-end tax reconciliation

Handled special cases:
- Dashain/performance bonuses
- Leave encashment & gratuity
- Final settlements for employees

**Outcome:** Accurate payroll execution with full tax compliance

---

### 3. Corporate Tax Compliance

- Prepared quarterly advance tax estimates
- Annual tax computation (profit reconciliation)
- Accounting vs taxable profit adjustments
- Deferred tax calculation (IAS 12)
- Filed corporate tax returns via IRD portal

**Outcome:** No tax penalties or interest exposure

---

### 4. SSF & PF Compliance

- Monthly SSF computation (employer + employee)
- Provident Fund contributions and settlements
- Timely remittance and reconciliation

---

### 5. TDS Reconciliation

Maintained structured reconciliation framework:
- Interest TDS vs General Ledger vs IRD records
- Salary TDS vs payroll system
- Vendor TDS vs payment records
- Monthly TDS liability reconciliation

**Outcome:** Clean audit-ready TDS ledger

---

### 6. Tax Return Filing & Documentation

- Annual corporate income tax filing
- Monthly TDS return filing
- Annual issuance of TDS certificates
- Full tax audit documentation pack maintenance

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes

- Zero tax penalties or interest charges
- 100% on-time IRD filings and payments
- Fully reconciled TDS across all categories
- Accurate payroll and statutory deductions
- Strong audit compliance record
