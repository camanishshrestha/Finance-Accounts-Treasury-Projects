## 🎯 Project Objective

Hospital procurement is a high-stakes function involving medical equipment, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, and services where poor decisions directly affect patient safety, quality of care, and cost efficiency.

As Team Leader, I supported OM Hospital in evaluating bids and vendor proposals to ensure transparent, fair, and value-driven procurement aligned with regulatory requirements.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: What I Did

---

### ✅ Step 1: Procurement Framework Understanding

Reviewed applicable procurement framework:

| Regulation | Applicability |
|---|---|
| Public Procurement Act (PPA) | Government-funded procurement |
| Public Procurement Regulations (PPR) | Procedural rules |
| Donor Guidelines | Override where donor-funded |
| Internal Policy | Hospital Board-approved rules |

---

### 📦 Procurement Categories Reviewed

- Medical equipment (CT, X-ray, ventilators)
- Pharmaceuticals & consumables
- IT systems (HIS, billing systems)
- Infrastructure works
- Services (security, cleaning, catering)
- Office supplies

---

### ✅ Step 2: Prequalification Evaluation

Assessed vendor eligibility before bidding stage:

**Criteria assessed:**
- Legal registration & tax compliance
- Financial strength (audited statements)
- Technical capability & manpower
- Past experience & references
- Manufacturer authorization (for medical equipment)
- ISO certifications
- Litigation/blacklisting status

**Output:**
- Scored vendors
- Filtered qualified bidders
- Prepared shortlist for bidding stage

---

### ✅ Step 3: Quotation Review

For low-value procurement:

- Verified minimum 3 quotations
- Checked authenticity (stamp, signature, letterhead)
- Ensured specification compliance
- Validated vendor eligibility
- Compared market pricing
- Detected suspicious similarities (collusion risk)

---

### ✅ Step 4: Comparative Bid Analysis

Prepared structured comparison:

- Technical compliance scoring
- Price comparison (total landed cost)
- Delivery timelines
- Warranty & service terms
- Payment conditions
- Vendor experience & certifications

**Outcome:**
Transparent ranking and recommendation based on objective scoring.

---

### ✅ Step 5: Medical Equipment Evaluation

Special focus on healthcare equipment:

- FDA / CE certification validation
- Technical performance review
- Installation requirements assessment
- Training & user support review
- Warranty + AMC evaluation
- Spare parts availability
- Service response capability
- Consumables cost analysis
- Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

> Insight: Lowest purchase price ≠ lowest lifetime cost.

---

### ✅ Step 6: Infrastructure & Service Evaluation

**Infrastructure:**
- BOQ verification
- Rate benchmarking vs market/district rates
- Contractor experience validation
- Timeline feasibility check
- Performance security requirement

**Services:**
- SLA definition (cleaning, security, waste management)
- Staffing adequacy review
- Compliance with labor laws
- Contract terms & escalation clauses

---

### ✅ Step 7: Integrity Checks

- Conflict of interest declarations
- Bid confidentiality control
- Collusion detection analysis
- Vendor blacklist screening
- Market price validation
- No vendor influence during evaluation

---

### ✅ Step 8: Final Evaluation Report

Prepared formal procurement report including:

- Procurement background
- Methodology
- Evaluation criteria
- Technical scoring
- Financial comparison
- Risk analysis
- Final recommendation
- Committee approval signatures
