# Project 3: IFRS/NFRS Financial Statement Preparation & Annual Report — Banking Sector

## 📌 Overview
This project covers end-to-end IFRS/NFRS financial reporting across two development banks in Nepal (2022–2025). As second-in-command (often Acting HOD), I led preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements along with full Annual Reports, ensuring compliance with IFRS, NRB, SEBON, and NEPSE requirements.

## 🎯 Objective
- Ensure accurate and compliant financial reporting  
- Deliver timely regulatory filings and disclosures  
- Enhance transparency and reporting quality  
- Support audit readiness and stakeholder reporting  

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed
- Led monthly financial closing (EIR interest, ECL provisions, accruals, depreciation)  
- Prepared quarterly IFRS/NFRS financial statements and regulatory filings  
- Executed year-end closing including ECL models, deferred tax, and actuarial adjustments  
- Managed General Ledger, journal entries, and reconciliations  
- Performed financial statement review (accuracy, consistency, disclosures)  
- Prepared Annual Reports (Annual Book) including financials, governance, and MD&A  
- Coordinated with external auditors and managed audit requirements  
- Posted financials in Core Banking Systems (Pumori, Finacle)  
- Ensured timely website publication and regulatory compliance  

## 📊 Key Outcomes
- Financial statements delivered: Monthly, Quarterly, Annual (2 banks)  
- 3 Annual Reports prepared and published  
- ICAN Best Presented Annual Report — Bronze (2 consecutive years)  
- Timely filings with NRB, SEBON, NEPSE  
- Smooth audits with minimal adjustments  

## 🧰 Tools & Skills
- IFRS/NFRS Financial Reporting  
- IFRS 9 (ECL), IAS 12, IAS 19  
- Financial Closing & GL Management  
- Annual Report Preparation  
- External Audit Coordination  
- Core Banking Systems (Pumori, Finacle)  
- Microsoft Excel, Power BI
