## 🎯 Project Objective

Led the full lifecycle execution of a **debenture issuance (LBBLD89)** — from financial modeling and regulatory approvals to SEBON filing, public subscription, and NEPSE listing.

This was a **high-complexity capital markets transaction** requiring coordination across finance, legal, regulatory, and market infrastructure institutions.

## 🛠️ Key Work Breakdown

---

### 1. Regulatory & Framework Understanding
- Studied Companies Act, SEBON regulations, NEPSE listing rules, NRB directives
- Mapped full issuance lifecycle and compliance requirements

---

### 2. Board & NRB Approval
- Prepared Board approval documents and issuance structure
- Managed NRB no-objection process for Tier 2 capital inclusion

---

### 3. Credit Rating Coordination
- Coordinated with SEBON-approved credit rating agency
- Provided financials, portfolio data, and risk profile
- Supported rating committee review and final rating outcome

---

### 4. 10-Year Financial Projections
- Built long-term projections for:
  - Balance Sheet
  - Income Statement
  - Cash Flow
- Included DSCR analysis and capital adequacy projections
- Conducted sensitivity (base, optimistic, pessimistic, stress scenarios)

---

### 5. Prospectus Preparation
- Prepared SEBON-compliant debenture prospectus covering:
  - Financial statements
  - Risk factors
  - Use of proceeds
  - Capital adequacy impact
  - Subscription terms
- Coordinated legal, audit, and rating disclosures

---

### 6. SEBON Filing & Approval
- Submitted full regulatory package to SEBON
- Responded to queries and revised prospectus accordingly
- Obtained final approval for public issuance

---

### 7. Trustee & RTS Management
- Appointed debenture trustee and executed trust deed
- Coordinated RTS (Registrar to Securities) for:
  - Subscription handling
  - Allotment
  - Refunds
  - Investor records

---

### 8. Public Subscription & Allotment
- Managed public issue process across branches and RTS channels
- Monitored subscription status and oversubscription handling
- Coordinated allotment and refund process

---

### 9. NEPSE Listing
- Prepared NEPSE listing application and agreement
- Ensured DEMAT credit via CDSC
- Enabled secondary market trading of debenture

---

### 10. Post-Listing Compliance
- Managed ongoing obligations:
  - Interest payments
  - SEBON & NEPSE reporting
  - Trustee reporting
  - TDS compliance
  - Investor record maintenance

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes

- Successful issuance and listing of **LBBLD89 debenture**
- Strengthened **Tier 2 capital position**
- Completed **end-to-end capital market transaction lifecycle**
- Ensured full compliance across SEBON, NRB, NEPSE
- Managed 13+ stakeholder groups simultaneously
- Zero regulatory non-compliance issues
