## 🎯 Project Objective

Statutory audit of a commercial bank is one of the **most complex audit engagements** in the profession — requiring deep understanding of banking operations, financial instruments, regulatory requirements, and IT systems. As a **team member** during my articleship and audit associate period, I participated in the statutory audit of **Sanima Bank Ltd** — one of Nepal's leading commercial banks — gaining hands-on experience in **risk-based audit planning, substantive testing, internal control evaluation, and audit reporting** for a large-scale banking institution.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: What I Did

---

### ✅ Step 1: Audit Planning & Risk Assessment

- Participated in the **audit planning phase**:
  - Studied the bank's **business model, organizational structure, and regulatory environment**
  - Reviewed **prior year audit files** — understanding prior findings, risk areas, and management responses
  - Assisted in developing the **risk-based audit plan**:
    - Identified significant accounts and assertions
    - Assessed **inherent risk** and **control risk** for each significant area
    - Determined **materiality levels** — overall materiality and performance materiality
    - Planned **audit procedures** — nature, timing, and extent of testing
  - Understood the bank's **IT environment** — Core Banking System (CBS), network infrastructure, key applications

---

### ✅ Step 2: Internal Control Evaluation

- Evaluated the **design and operating effectiveness** of internal controls:

#### 🔹 Walkthrough Procedures
- Traced sample transactions from initiation to recording:
  - **Loan origination** → approval → disbursement → accounting
  - **Deposit acceptance** → CBS entry → interest computation → accounting
  - **Payment processing** → authorization → settlement → recording

#### 🔹 Key Control Testing
| Control Type | Testing Approach |
|--------------|-----------------|
| **Authorization Controls** | Verified proper approval at prescribed levels per delegation of authority |
| **Segregation of Duties** | Tested maker-checker compliance across critical processes |
| **Reconciliation Controls** | Verified timeliness and accuracy of account reconciliations |
| **IT General Controls** | Reviewed access management, change management, backup procedures |

- Documented **control deficiencies** identified during walkthroughs and testing
- Assessed impact of control deficiencies on **substantive audit procedures** — weak controls required more extensive substantive testing

---

### ✅ Step 3: Loan Portfolio Audit

- Assisted in auditing the bank's **loan portfolio** — the largest and most material asset:

#### 🔹 Loan File Testing Framework
- Selected **sample of loan files** using risk-based and statistical sampling methods
- For each sampled loan file, verified:

| Verification Area | Key Checks Performed |
|------------------|---------------------|
| **Loan Sanction** | Proper credit appraisal, CIB report, financial analysis, risk grading |
| **Approval** | Authorized at appropriate level per credit policy and delegation matrix |
| **Documentation** | Loan agreement, promissory note, collateral documents, KYC completeness |
| **Collateral** | Valuation report validity, mortgage registration, insurance adequacy and currency |
| **Disbursement** | Proper disbursement to correct account, pre-disbursement conditions met |
| **Classification** | NRB classification accuracy (Pass/Watch/Substandard/Doubtful/Loss) |
| **Provisioning** | NRB provision and IFRS 9 ECL computation accuracy |
| **Interest Accrual** | EIR-based interest computation, suspension on NPL accounts |
| **Renewal/Review** | Timely renewal for revolving facilities, periodic credit review |

---

### ✅ Step 4: Deposit & Liability Verification

- Assisted in auditing the bank's **deposit liabilities**:

#### 🔹 Deposit Balance Verification
- Verified **deposit balances** through:
  - CBS report extraction and reconciliation with General Ledger
  - Sample-based **deposit confirmation** — sending confirmation letters to depositors
  - Review of **large and unusual deposit transactions** for proper authorization

#### 🔹 Interest Expense Testing
- Verified **interest expense accuracy**:
  - Recalculated interest on sample deposits using contractual rates
  - Verified interest rates against Board/ALCO-approved rate schedules
  - Checked TDS deduction accuracy on deposit interest per Income Tax Act

#### 🔹 Borrowings Verification
- Verified **borrowings**:
  - Interbank borrowings — confirmation with counterparty banks
  - NRB facility utilizations — SLF/SDF/OLF records reconciliation
  - Debenture liabilities — terms, coupon payments, outstanding balance

---

### ✅ Step 5: Investment Portfolio Audit

- Assisted in auditing the bank's **investment portfolio**:

#### 🔹 Government Securities (T-Bills, Bonds)
- Verified holdings against NRB records / custodian statements
- Recalculated EIR-based interest accrual
- Verified IFRS 9 classification (Amortized Cost / FVOCI) and measurement

#### 🔹 Equity Investments
- Verified holdings against DEMAT / CDSC statements
- Verified fair value measurement — NEPSE market price at reporting date
- Checked classification (FVTPL / FVOCI) and accounting treatment per IFRS 9

#### 🔹 Interbank Placements
- Sent **interbank confirmations** to counterparty banks
- Verified placement amounts, interest rates, and maturity dates
- Reconciled interbank balances with CBS and counterparty statements

---

### ✅ Step 6: Revenue & Expense Testing

- Performed **substantive testing** on key income and expense line items:

#### 🔹 Interest Income Testing
- Recalculated interest on sample loans using EIR method
- Verified interest suspension on NPL accounts per accounting policy
- Analytical review — comparing interest income to average earning assets × average yield

#### 🔹 Fee & Commission Income Testing
- Vouched sample fee income transactions to source documents (invoices, agreements)
- Verified fee rates against Board-approved fee schedule
- Tested cut-off — fees recorded in correct accounting period

#### 🔹 Operating Expenses Testing
- Vouched sample expense transactions to invoices, contracts, and payment records
- Verified expense authorization at appropriate levels per delegation policy
- Tested for **cut-off accuracy** — expenses recorded in correct period

#### 🔹 Staff Costs Testing
- Verified sample salaries against HR records and appointment letters
- Checked PF/SSF contributions against statutory requirements
- Verified bonus provision computation per regulatory guidelines

---

### ✅ Step 7: Analytical Procedures

- Performed **analytical procedures** to identify unusual trends and potential misstatements:

#### 🔹 Trend & Ratio Analysis
| Analysis Type | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| **Trend Analysis** | Comparing current year balances and ratios with prior years to identify anomalies |
| **Ratio Analysis** | NIM, cost-to-income, NPL ratio, provision coverage, CAR — benchmarking and trend review |
| **Reasonableness Checks** | Interest income ≈ Average loans × Average yield; Interest expense ≈ Average deposits × Average cost |

#### 🔹 Variance Investigation
- Investigated **significant variances** from expected results:
  - Obtained explanations from management
  - Corroborated explanations with supporting evidence
  - Assessed whether variances indicated potential misstatement or legitimate business factors

---

### ✅ Step 8: Regulatory Compliance Verification

- Verified the bank's compliance with **key NRB regulatory requirements**:

#### 🔹 Key Regulatory Areas Tested

| Regulatory Requirement | Verification Approach |
|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Capital Adequacy (CAR)** | Verified CAR computation accuracy — Tier 1, Tier 2, RWA calculations |
| **CRR/SLR Compliance** | Verified daily/periodic compliance with minimum reserve requirements |
| **Single Obligor Limit (SOL)** | Checked for SOL breaches — individual and group exposure limits |
| **Sector-wise Lending Limits** | Verified compliance with NRB sectoral concentration limits |
| **Deprived Sector Lending** | Verified compliance with priority lending targets and reporting |
| **Related Party Lending** | Verified compliance with NRB related party exposure limits and disclosure |
| **Loan Classification & Provisioning** | Verified NRB directive compliance on classification and minimum provisions |

---

### ✅ Step 9: Audit Completion & Reporting

- Assisted in **audit completion procedures**:

#### 🔹 Completion Procedures
| Procedure | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Subsequent Events Review** | Reviewed events after reporting date per IAS 10 — adjusting vs. non-adjusting |
| **Going Concern Assessment** | Evaluated bank's ability to continue operations for foreseeable future |
| **Management Representation Letter** | Obtained written representations from management on key assertions |
| **Audit Adjustments** | Proposed and recorded audit adjustments for identified misstatements |
| **Summary of Unadjusted Differences** | Documented immaterial unadjusted items with rationale |

#### 🔹 Audit Report Contribution
- Assisted in preparing the **audit report** including:
  - Auditor's opinion on financial statements (unqualified/qualified/adverse/disclaimer)
  - Key Audit Matters (KAMs) — areas of significant audit focus and response
  - Management Letter — internal control observations and recommendations for improvement
