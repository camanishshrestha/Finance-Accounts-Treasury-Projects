## 🎯 Project Objective

Statutory audit of a commercial bank is one of the **most complex audit engagements** in the profession — requiring deep understanding of banking operations, financial instruments, regulatory requirements, and IT systems.

As a **team member**, I participated in the statutory audit of **Sanima Bank Ltd**, gaining hands-on experience in:
- Risk-based audit planning  
- Substantive testing  
- Internal control evaluation  
- Audit reporting

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities

### 1. Audit Planning & Risk Assessment
- Reviewed prior audit files and identified risk areas  
- Assisted in risk-based audit planning and materiality setting  
- Understood business model and IT environment (CBS)  

### 2. Internal Control Evaluation
- Performed walkthroughs (loan, deposit, payment cycles)  
- Tested:
  - Authorization controls  
  - Segregation of duties  
  - Reconciliation controls  
  - IT general controls  
- Documented control deficiencies  

### 3. Loan Portfolio Audit
- Verified loan sanction, approval, documentation, collateral  
- Checked classification (NRB) and provisioning (NRB + IFRS 9)  
- Recalculated interest using EIR  

### 4. Deposit & Liability Verification
- Reconciled CBS with GL  
- Performed deposit confirmations  
- Verified interest expense and TDS  
- Reviewed borrowings  

### 5. Investment Audit
- Verified government securities and equity investments  
- Checked fair value (NEPSE) and IFRS classification  
- Performed interbank confirmations  

### 6. Revenue & Expense Testing
- Tested interest income, fee income, operating expenses  
- Verified authorization and cut-off  
- Checked staff costs, PF/SSF, bonus  

### 7. Analytical Procedures
- Performed trend and ratio analysis (NIM, NPL, CAR)  
- Conducted reasonableness checks  
- Investigated variances  

### 8. Regulatory Compliance
- Verified compliance with:
  - CAR  
  - CRR/SLR  
  - SOL  
  - Sector lending limits  
  - Loan classification & provisioning  

### 9. Audit Completion & Reporting
- Subsequent events and going concern review  
- Assisted in audit report and KAMs  
- Prepared management letter observations
