## 🎯 Project Objective

Budgeting in banking is a strategic financial planning process that determines resource allocation, cost control, and profitability targets.

This project involved:
- Annual & quarterly budget preparation
- Variance analysis (budget vs actual)
- Cost control and efficiency tracking
- Implementation of Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB) at institutional level

ZBB was introduced to eliminate historical inefficiencies and enforce **cost justification for every expense from zero base**.

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed

### 1. Budget Framework Understanding
- Analyzed incremental budgeting limitations
- Aligned budgeting process with NRB guidelines
- Identified inefficiencies in historical cost-based budgeting

---

### 2. Annual Budget Preparation
Prepared full financial budget including:
- Interest income (loans, investments, interbank)
- Interest expense (deposits, borrowings)
- Non-interest income (fees, commissions, FX income)
- Operating expenses (staff, admin, IT, marketing)
- Profit forecasting (NII, NPAT)

---

### 3. Balance Sheet Budgeting
- Loan portfolio projection
- Deposit growth forecasting
- Investment portfolio planning
- Capital adequacy (CAR) projection

---

### 4. CAPEX Budgeting
- Branch expansion planning
- IT infrastructure upgrades
- ATM, vehicles, and equipment investments
- ROI and justification analysis

---

### 5. Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB) Implementation

**Core transformation project**

Steps:
- Introduced ZBB concept to management & Board
- Defined decision units across departments & branches
- Created decision packages for all expenses:
  - Purpose
  - Cost
  - Benefit
  - Justification
  - Alternatives
- Ranked all expenses by priority
- Allocated funds only above “funding line”

Result:
- Eliminated non-value-adding expenses
- Improved cost discipline
- Strengthened accountability

---

### 6. Quarterly Budget & Rolling Forecasts
- Quarterly budget breakdown
- Seasonal adjustments (festival, fiscal cycles)
- Rolling forecast updates based on actual performance

---

### 7. Budget Variance Analysis
Analyzed:
- Revenue variance (interest & non-interest)
- Expense variance
- Profit variance
- Balance sheet deviations

Performed:
- Root cause analysis
- Controllable vs uncontrollable classification
- Corrective action recommendations

---

### 8. Cost Control & Efficiency Monitoring
Tracked:
- Cost-to-income ratio
- Staff cost ratio
- IT cost efficiency
- Revenue per employee/branch

Identified:
- Cost reduction opportunities
- Process inefficiencies
- Automation potential

---

### 9. Budget Communication & Execution
- Issued budget circulars
- Set departmental & branch targets
- Conducted performance alignment meetings
- Monitored execution against budget

---

### 10. Regulatory & Board Reporting
- Prepared budget submission for NRB
- Delivered Board budget presentations
- Maintained audit-ready documentation and assumptions file
