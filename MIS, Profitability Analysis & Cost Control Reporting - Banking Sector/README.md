## 🎯 Project Objective

MIS reporting transforms financial data into **decision-making intelligence**.

This project focused on:
- Branch & product profitability analysis
- Cost control and efficiency tracking
- KPI dashboard design
- Management reporting for CEO and Board decisions

The goal was to enable **data-driven banking decisions**, not just reporting numbers.

> ⚠️ Note: All institution-specific data and financial figures are confidential.

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed

### 1. MIS Framework Design
- Designed full MIS reporting structure
- Defined report frequency, users, and format
- Built standardized reporting templates

Reports included:
- Branch profitability
- Product profitability
- KPI dashboards
- Cost analysis
- Budget variance reports

---

### 2. Branch Profitability Analysis
- Built full-cost allocation model
- Applied **Funds Transfer Pricing (FTP)** system
- Allocated:
  - Revenue (loans, fees, interest)
  - Direct costs (staff, rent, operations)
  - Indirect costs (HO allocation)

Outcome:
- Identified profitable and loss-making branches
- Supported branch expansion/consolidation decisions

---

### 3. Product Profitability Analysis
Analyzed profitability of:
- Loan products (housing, SME, personal, agriculture)
- Deposit products (CASA, fixed deposits, call deposits)
- Fee-based services (remittance, trade finance, cards)

Key outputs:
- Risk-adjusted return analysis
- Product-wise margin evaluation
- Portfolio optimization recommendations

---

### 4. Cost of Funds & Yield Analysis
- Cost of funds by product, branch, and customer segment
- Yield on assets by loan/investment type
- Net Interest Margin (NIM) decomposition

Delivered:
- Cost drivers identification
- Yield compression analysis
- Profitability optimization insights

---

### 5. KPI Dashboard Development
Designed management dashboards covering:

**Financial KPIs**
- ROA, ROE, NIM, EPS

**Efficiency KPIs**
- Cost-to-Income Ratio
- Revenue per Employee/Branch

**Risk KPIs**
- NPL ratio
- CAR
- Provision coverage

**Liquidity KPIs**
- CRR, SLR, LCR, NSFR

---

### 6. Cost Control & Monitoring
- Department-wise expense tracking
- Budget vs actual monitoring
- Cost-saving opportunity identification
- Vendor and operational cost optimization

---

### 7. Peer Benchmarking
- Compared performance with peer banks
- Benchmarked:
  - Profitability
  - Efficiency
  - Asset quality
  - Capital strength
  - Growth metrics

Used insights to recommend strategic improvements.

---

### 8. Management Reporting
Prepared monthly MIS packs:
- Income statement analysis
- Balance sheet summary
- KPI dashboard
- Branch performance ranking
- Key insights & recommendations

---

### 9. MIS Automation
- Built Excel-based automated reporting systems
- Used pivot tables, formulas, and dashboards
- Explored Power BI visualization tools
- Reduced reporting time significantly

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes

- Built structured MIS reporting system from scratch
- Implemented branch & product profitability analysis
- Enabled KPI-based performance tracking for Board decisions
- Strengthened cost control and efficiency monitoring
- Improved management decision-making speed and quality
