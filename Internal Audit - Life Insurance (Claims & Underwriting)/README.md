## 🎯 Project Objective

Life insurance deals with **high-impact financial events** — especially death and maturity claims. Weak controls can lead to fraud, delays, or reputational damage.  
This project focused on **auditing claims processing and underwriting** to ensure accuracy, compliance, and efficiency.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: What I Did

---

### ✅ Step 1: Understanding Operations

- Studied core functions:
  - Underwriting (risk selection)
  - Claims processing
  - Premium collection
  - Reinsurance & investment
- Reviewed regulatory framework:
  - Insurance Act
  - Insurance Board guidelines
  - IAIS principles
  - AML requirements

---

### ✅ Step 2: Audit Planning

- Identified key risks:

| Area | Risks |
|---|---|
| Claims | Fraud, delays, wrong payments |
| Underwriting | Poor risk assessment, wrong pricing |

- Defined objectives:
  - Accuracy, timeliness, compliance
  - Strong authorization & documentation

---

### ✅ Step 3: Death Claim Audit

- Verified:
  - Death certificate authenticity
  - Policy validity
  - Nominee legitimacy
  - Medical vs declared history

- Focus areas:
  - Early claims (high fraud risk)
  - Suicide clause compliance

---

### ✅ Step 4: Maturity & Survival Claims

- Recalculated:
  - Maturity value = Sum Assured + Bonus
- Verified:
  - Premium completeness
  - Payment authorization
  - Identity validation

---

### ✅ Step 5: Surrender Claims

- Checked:
  - Surrender value (GSV vs SSV)
  - Eligibility period
  - Loan deductions
  - Proper approvals

---

### ✅ Step 6: Payment Authorization

- Ensured:
  - Approval hierarchy followed
  - No unauthorized payments
  - Correct beneficiary paid
  - Proper recording in system

---

### ✅ Step 7: TAT Analysis

- Formula:
TAT = Settlement Date – Intimation Date


- Findings:
- Delays due to documentation gaps, internal inefficiencies

- Output:
- TAT report + improvement recommendations

---

### ✅ Step 8: Underwriting Review

- Verified:
- Proposal completeness
- Medical checks
- Income vs sum assured
- Premium accuracy
- Risk classification

- Risk focus:
- Adverse selection
- Non-disclosure
- Agent-driven fraud patterns

---

### ✅ Step 9: MIS & Claims Register

- Checked:
- Completeness of claims register
- Accuracy of outstanding claims
- IBNR provisions
- MIS metrics (settlement ratio, rejection ratio)

---

### ✅ Step 10: Reporting

- Delivered:
- Risk-based findings (🔴🟡🟢)
- Root cause analysis
- Practical recommendations

- Presented to:
- Senior Management
- Audit Committee
