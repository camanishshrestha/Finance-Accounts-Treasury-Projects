## 🎯 Project Objective

Save the Children is one of the world's largest child-focused international organizations operating in over 100 countries. The audit focuses not only on financial compliance but also on program effectiveness, safeguarding requirements, and multi-donor accountability.

The core objective was:
**“Were donor funds used for intended child-focused programs in compliance with donor agreements and internal controls?”**

As Team Leader/Team Member, I conducted audits ensuring funds were properly utilized, partners complied with requirements, and child safeguarding standards were maintained.

### ✅ Step 1: Understanding Program Structure

- Reviewed organizational structure:
  - Global HQ (Save the Children International)
  - Country Office (Nepal)
  - Field Offices
  - Implementing Partners
  - Donor-funded programs (multi-donor environment)

  - Education

  - Humanitarian Response

### ✅ Step 2: Audit Planning

- Reviewed program documents and donor agreements:
  - Approved budgets
  - Work plans
  - Donor requirements
- Defined audit scope:
  - Programs and partners covered
  - Period under review
  - Risk-based sample selection

- Key risks identified:
  - Misallocation of donor funds
  - Weak partner controls
  - Documentation gaps
  - Fraud risk in field operations
