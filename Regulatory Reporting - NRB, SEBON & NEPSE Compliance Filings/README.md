# Project 4: Regulatory Reporting — NRB, SEBON & NEPSE Compliance Filings

## 📌 Overview
This project covers end-to-end regulatory reporting and compliance filings for two development banks in Nepal (2022–2025). The role involved ensuring accurate, timely, and fully compliant submissions to Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB), Securities Board of Nepal (SEBON), and Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE).

## 🎯 Objective
- Ensure full compliance with regulatory reporting requirements  
- Maintain accuracy and timeliness of all submissions  
- Strengthen governance and regulatory transparency  
- Prevent penalties through proactive compliance monitoring  

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed
- Managed NRB SIS Portal reporting as designated Checker  
- Verified and submitted Capital Adequacy (Basel II/III) reports  
- Prepared CRR, SLR, LCR, and NSFR liquidity reports  
- Performed loan classification, provisioning, and asset quality reporting  
- Submitted interest rate risk and off-balance sheet exposure reports  
- Prepared SEBON quarterly/annual financial disclosures and compliance filings  
- Managed NEPSE disclosures including financial notes, corporate actions, and shareholder data  
- Maintained regulatory reporting calendar for all submission deadlines  
- Performed CBS reconciliations with NRB accounts  
- Coordinated responses during NRB regulatory inspections  

## 📊 Key Outcomes
- Zero missed regulatory deadlines across NRB, SEBON, and NEPSE  
- Accurate capital adequacy and liquidity compliance maintained  
- Seamless coordination with regulators during inspections  
- Strong internal compliance tracking and reporting system established  

## 🧰 Tools & Skills
- NRB SIS Portal & Reporting Systems  
- Basel II/III Capital Adequacy Framework  
- CRR / SLR / LCR / NSFR Computation  
- SEBON & NEPSE Filing Systems  
- Loan Classification & Provisioning  
- Regulatory Compliance Dashboards  
- Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
