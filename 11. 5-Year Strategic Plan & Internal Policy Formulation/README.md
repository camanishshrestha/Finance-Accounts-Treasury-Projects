## 🛠️ Key Work (Simplified)

### 1. Situational Analysis
- Financial review (ROA, ROE, NIM, NPL, CAR)
- Operational review (branches, tech, HR, products)
- SWOT + PESTEL analysis
- Competitor benchmarking

---

### 2. Strategic Framework
- Vision → Mission → Pillars → Objectives → KPIs → Action Plans → Monitoring


#### Strategic Pillars:
- Growth
- Profitability
- Risk Management
- Digital Transformation
- Expansion
- HR Development
- Governance
- Financial Inclusion

### 3. Financial Projections (5-Year)
- Balance Sheet growth (Loans, Deposits, Assets)
- Profit projections (NII, NPAT)
- Ratio targets (ROA, ROE, NPL, CAR)
- Scenario analysis (Base / Best / Worst)
